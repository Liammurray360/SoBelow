Where It Started

So Below Entertainment officially kicked off in 2022. Nothing fancy, just a few alternative shows, a lot of hard work, and a genuine love for live music.

Not long after, we launched State Of The Nation, which became our biggest show brand and a big part of who we are.

Scene First

We’ve always been about the scene first. Good shows, solid sound, proper lighting, fair treatment of bands, and an environment where people actually want to come back. No egos.

Now we’re expanding.

More Space To Grow

So Below is opening things up to more genres, more sounds, more venues and more artists from all over the country.

More opportunities for bands, more variety for crowds, and more room for scenes to thrive without stepping on each other.

The Goal

At the end of the day, we’re here to build something sustainable, exciting, and real.

Put on great events and support the people who make live music what it is.

From the underground, and everywhere else we can take it.
