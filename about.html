About So Below

A collective built for loud rooms, heavier lineups, and nights that don’t behave.

So Below Entertainment is an independent event collective based in Johannesburg, focused on building real, high-energy live music experiences.

We work with local artists across metal, alternative, acoustic, and everything in between, putting together shows that actually feel alive. No copy-paste lineups, no safe curation. Just properly put together nights that hit hard.

From intimate acoustic sets to full-scale multi-stage metal events, every show is built with intention. Sound, lighting, flow, lineup pacing, all of it matters.

Real Lineups

Carefully built bills that make sense musically and energetically. Not just throwing bands together and hoping it works.

Proper Production

Clean sound, tight stage management, and attention to detail. Because a good band still sounds bad on a bad setup.

Actual Community

Supporting artists, connecting crowds, and growing something sustainable instead of chasing one-off hype.

If you’re a band, venue, or just want in on the chaos, let’s talk.
