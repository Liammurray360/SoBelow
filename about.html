Where It Started

So Below Entertainment kicked off in 2022. No big plan — just putting on shows that actually felt right.

From there, State Of The Nation took shape and became the flagship — louder, heavier, and bigger every time.

Scene First

The focus has always been the same — build shows people actually want to be at. Good sound, proper production, strong lineups, and a crowd that’s there for the music.

Now we’re expanding.

More Space To Grow
